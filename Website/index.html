Job specification & required qualifications

  • Specification

    You will be responsible for website design and producing draft sites that are user friendly and look good. You will present your work to clients and include any feedback to product an exceptional end product. This role also includes the responsability for the design, layout and coding of the Hot Beans website, focusing on the technical and graphical aspects. You will ensure the site is correctly formatted and be able to write the programming code. As a juniour web developer, you will be working alone, as part of a team and will meet with clients to discuss their projects.

    Hours are Monday to Friday 9am-5pm. Overtime is availbale if interested but would require working on weekends and evenings. Ther starting salary is £18,000 but is reviewed regularly and can increase to £40,000+. On the job training is included.

  • You Must

    Have skills in programming and graphics.

    Be creative and use your imagination.

    Be able to adapt to new techniques, and develop skills in relevant software and programming. This includes both programming languages and image editing.

    Have good communication skills.

    Keep up to date with advances in computer technology and software developments, and acknowledge how this affects the business.

    Be interested in the internet.

    Have experience in troubleshooting and problem solving.

  • Qualifications

    Four GCSE's grades A-C.

    BTEC National Diploma in Art and Design (Multimedia), Computer studies or IT.

    IT Qualification level 2 - 4.

    Degrees in IT, Computer science, programming, software engineering and other subjects.
